About Me

I am a born and raised Bay Area native that has always had a passion for technology. From a young age I was very aware of the power of the ever-expanding world of tech. Through technology, we can learn, teach, explore, collaborate, and investigate among other things. In the age of information, the possiblities are endless.

I attended UC Berkeley and graduated with a Bachelors in Linguistic Science, but learning about human language wasn't enough. I found my love for coding and problem solving 4 years later, and become a certified MERN Full Stack Web Devloper through UC Berkeley extension in 2018.

It'd be a pleasure to work with you.
